Nutrition

One app for the session and the plate.

Daily macros

Calories, protein, carbs, fat — at a glance.

Targets calibrated to your goals, body stats, and weekly training volume. Daily rings tell you exactly where you stand before your next meal.

  • Photo log. Snap your plate. Macros get estimated and you confirm before saving.
  • Barcode scan. Point at any label. Calories and macros populate instantly.
  • USDA-backed search. Branded foods, restaurant chains, and barcodes — search and save in two taps.
  • Saved meals & custom foods. Re-log a recurring meal in seconds. Define foods that aren't in the database.
  • Water tracking. Hydration sits beside macros, so it's part of the picture.

Logger

Five ways to log, one place to look.

Search, scan, snap, ask, or write — the food logger blends every entry method into one clean view. Pick the path that takes the fewest taps for what you're eating.

Weekly view

Nutrition trends, not just daily numbers.

Weekly average calories, macro splits, and consistency flow into the AI coach so its plans match how you actually eat — not how you say you eat. The picture gets sharper week over week.

Common questions

Answers before you ask.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. LiftAi Pro is billed through Apple. Cancel any time in iPhone Settings > your Apple ID > Subscriptions. You keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period — no clawback, no support call.

Will my workouts move to a new iPhone?

Yes. LiftAi syncs your workouts, templates, PRs, and nutrition through your account, so signing in on a new iPhone restores everything. Local-first means your data is also on-device, fast and offline-friendly.

Do I need an Apple Watch?

No. LiftAi runs entirely on iPhone. Apple Health integration is optional — if you have an Apple Watch or sleep tracker, the AI coach uses your readiness signals; if you don’t, the coach still works.

Can Free users still use the AI coach?

Yes. Free includes three AI coaching messages per day, plus unlimited workout tracking, the exercise library, stats, and achievements. Pro unlocks unlimited coaching, AI plan generation, stall detection, and advanced analytics.

What happens to my data if I cancel or delete my account?

Cancelling Pro keeps your account and all your workout history — you simply revert to the Free tier. Deleting your account through Profile > Delete Account permanently removes your data from our servers in two taps.

Is the AI coach medical advice?

No. LiftAi coaching is educational fitness guidance and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or a certified trainer. Always consult a healthcare provider before starting a new exercise program.
